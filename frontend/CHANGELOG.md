- Initial release of landing page
- Hero section with glassmorphism effects and gradient backgrounds
- Apple-style social dock with X, LinkedIn, and GitHub links
- Lead capture modal with premium glass effect
- Content sections showcasing AI-Native QA approach:
  - The AI-Native Shift (Autonomous, Intelligent, Predictive)
  - Tech Stack & Architecture (Playwright, AI Agents, CI/CD)
  - Leadership & Impact (Metrics: 90% faster feedback, 85% fewer incidents, 60% lower maintenance)
